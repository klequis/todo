# Styling

## Adjust font sizes

The heading for a card is a `h3` but the card contents can have all headings including `h1` and `h2` which are larger than the headings `h3`. This makes the relationship between the card heading and the card contents unbalanced and difficult to follow with the eye.

The card title should always have more emphasis than the card content and the card content font size whould never be larger than the card heading.

One suggestion is to modify the properties of headings for columns and cards.

- Card title can be `h1` but it will be smaller than `h1` on the page such as the page title "Todo Board"
- Card content can have `h1` but that `h1` will be smaller than the `h1` in the card title.
